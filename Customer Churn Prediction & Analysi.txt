The prediction of customer churn is an emerging area of interest in the domain of customer relationship management. In a competitive industry like telecommunications, if customers decide not to continue their services, it would cause substantial financial loss for companies. Prediction of customer churn helps companies identify customers who are likely to leave their services and take adequate measures to retain them. In recent times, due to the availability of large customer databases, machine learning is being used for analysis of customer data and prediction of patterns related to customer churn.This chapter reviews existing research related to customer churn prediction, machine learning techniques used for churn analysis, and the evaluation methods applied in previous studies. The aim of this literature review is to understand the current state of research, identify commonly used predictive models, and highlight limitations in existing studies that justify the need for this research (Chang, V et al, 2024).
